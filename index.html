Guruji Shri Sunil Patil

Surabhi Music Academy was founded in 2012 by Shri Sunil Patil honoring the age old lineage of Guru Shishya Parampara (teacher-disciple relationship). He belongs to Maihar Gharana, being a disciple of Shri Vivek Sonar (senior disciple of Pdt Hariprasad).

A virtuoso in both the mouth organ and flute, he has dedicated his life to music, teaching and the development of Mouth Organ. Being trained in Hindustani Classical Music since his childhood, he is currently researching on the development of Gayaki Angs like Meend and Gamaks on the mouth organ.

His love for Mouth organ gave birth to the annual Harmonica Festival that showcases the finest talents in the world of mouth organ. It not only promotes the Mouth Organ in the country but also provides students the stage to showcase their skills in front of a huge audience alongside of the masters.
